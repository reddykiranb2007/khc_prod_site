Support & AMC

Reliable respiratory care depends not only on device performance, but on consistent support throughout the product lifecycle. Kyron Healthcare provides structured service and maintenance programs to ensure predictable operation in clinical environments.

Our Support Philosophy

Kyron’s support model is designed around clinical continuity and uptime. Service processes are structured to minimize disruption, reduce downtime, and support safe clinical use.

Key Principles

  • Predictable response timelines
  • Clear escalation pathways
  • Local service capability
  • Transparent communication

Service Coverage

Kyron provides structured service coverage across installation, operation, and maintenance phases.

Installation and Commissioning

Ensuring correct setup, calibration, and environmental compatibility.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections and part replacements to proactively manage wear.

Corrective Service & Repairs

Efficient diagnosis and resolution of operational issues.

Software & Firmware Updates

Deployment of relevant updates to maintain system performance and safety.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Annual Maintenance Contracts are designed to provide predictable service coverage and cost control for healthcare facilities.

AMC Offerings Typically Include:

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance visits
  • Priority service response
  • Replacement of wear components as defined
  • Documentation and service records

Response & Escalation

Service requests are handled through a structured escalation framework to ensure timely resolution.

01

Acknowledgment

Receipt of request and initial categorization.

02

Assessment

Technical triage to determine likely cause and parts needs.

03

Resolution

Remote guidance or on-site engineer deployment.

04

Escalation

Higher-level technical support for complex issues.

Training & Handover

Effective support begins with correct installation and training. Kyron provides structured handover and training to clinical and biomedical teams during deployment.

Operator Familiarization

Focusing on daily use, alarms, and basic care.

Biomedical Training

Focusing on maintenance, troubleshooting, and safety checks.

Documentation Handover

Delivery of manuals, checklists, and warranty records.
