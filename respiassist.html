RespiAssist

Non-invasive ventilation system designed for neonatal and pediatric care.

Key Features

  • • NIV therapies suitable for neonatal & pediatric patients
  • • Precise FiO₂ and flow delivery
  • • Built-in blower for consistent air supply
  • • Smart alarm with audio-visual indicators
  • • Compact, easy-to-use NIV platform
  • • Clear, integrated display
  • • Designed for NICU & PICU use

Technical Specifications

Display 5-inch touch display
Therapy Modes nCPAP, Bubble CPAP, HFNC/O₂
FiO₂ Range 21 – 100 %
Flow Range 4 – 25 / 40 LPM
Pressure (PEEP) 1 – 10 cm H₂O
Monitoring FiO₂, Flow, Pressure, Alarms
Power 100–265 V AC
