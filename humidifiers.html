KHC Humidifier Series

Precise, consistent humidification supporting safer neonatal and pediatric respiratory care.

View KHC 1000 View KHC 3000

Why humidification matters

Delivering warmed and humidified gases helps protect delicate airways, improves comfort, and supports effective therapy — especially for neonates.

KHC 1000 — Respiratory Humidifier

  • • Full servo-controlled heating
  • • Multiple therapy modes
  • • Bright 7-segment temperature display
  • • Accurate temperature control

Key Specifications

  • — Heater plate capacity: 150W (max 115°C)
  • — Temperature range: 5 to 70°C (±0.3°C within 25–45°C)
  • — Recommended ambient: 18–26°C

Technical Specifications

Dimensions W135 × D170 × H156 mm
Weight 2.9 kg (without chamber)
Supply Voltage 220 V, 50/60 Hz
Current 3 A (max)
Heater Plate Capacity 150 W — up to 115 °C
Temperature Range 5 – 70 °C (±0.3 °C from 25 – 45 °C)
Flow Range Invasive: up to 40 L/min
Non-invasive: up to 60 L/min
Warm-up Time Less than 30 minutes
Standards IEC 60601-1, IEC 60601-1-2, ISO 8185
